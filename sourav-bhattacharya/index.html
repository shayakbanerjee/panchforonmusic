Sourav Bhattacharya

I am the songwriter and part time percussionist for the band. I find in Panchforon an expression of myself both as an individual and as a part of my community – the Bengali diaspora. Our songs (both originals and reimaginations) capture our shared history of growing up in urban Bengal with its sights, sounds and stories and then crossing the seven seas to live, but never quite settle, in the proverbial elsewhere. We simultaneously look back and look forward, hanging between multiple worlds. Panchforon is my search for my constantly shifting identity.
